Who we are

WYK was created with a simple mission to help under-represented young people launch careers in digital while supporting businesses in widening their talent pool.

Since starting in 2019, we have been focused on supporting young people to realise their career potential whilst helping businesses de-risk hiring entry-level talent and supporting the wider digital and tech industries to become more diverse.
